# Code of Conduct

The Lattice project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**.

We are committed to a harassment-free, welcoming, and inclusive experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We expect participants to be respectful, assume good faith, and focus on what is best for the standard and its community.

The full text is at: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

**Reporting:** until the project moves to a foundation with its own process, conduct concerns can be raised privately with the maintainers (the PredBat team). Reports will be handled confidentially.

(On extraction to a dedicated repository, the full Contributor Covenant text and a named contact are included here.)
